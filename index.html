What is this?

Scrolr is a Chrome extension which will change the way we browse the Internet currently. At present, we need to physically use a mouse or keyboard to scroll or interact on a webpage, however, with scrolr we wen use our body gestures for doing the same thing!

Features
  • Move head up and down for scrolling pages up and down.
  • Move head left and right for going back and forward.
  • Move head slightly left and right for horizontal scrolling.
  • Use hands for 'pinch and zoom' feature on the webpage.

NOTE: All of these features are not enabled yet.
